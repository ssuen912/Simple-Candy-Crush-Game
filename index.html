Instructions

This is a simplified Candy Crush/ Bejeweled like game. Line up three or more in a row/ column to earn points.

Each fruit worth 100 points. The more fruits you match in a row/ column, the more points you earn.

Note that you can keep moving until there are matches.

You may also choose to "Refresh Broad" if you believe that there are no more moves. "Refresh Broad" will cost 2 moves."

If you finish the level with less moves, you will gain extra points. Please use your moves wisely.

There are total 5 levels. Each level has their own target scores and move limits.

GOOD LUCK and HAVE FUN!!!
